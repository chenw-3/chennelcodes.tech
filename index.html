Learn About Me

Data Analyst

After graduating with a B.A in Biology in 2019, I wasn't sure if I wanted to pursue a Graduate degree or keep searching for opportunities within my field. My curiosity led me to learning Python as a hobby to build cool things, and I fell in love with it! My new found love led me to discovering Data Science & Analytics and, to my surprise, it was a perfect fit for my personality. As a naturally curious and intuitive person, Data Science allows me to use my natural talents to uncover insights buried deep in data.

My Journey

My journey to becoming a Data Analyst

Feb 2020

Started learning Python

Minot, ND

I was first introduced to Python in my college Bioinformatics class so I figured I'd give it another shot. After hours of scouring the plethora of online courses that were available, I decided to commit Udacity's Intro to Python course to learn the basics. P.S. It's free and I highly recommend it!

Oct 2020

Committed to learning Data Science & Analytics

Kingston, Jamaica

After moving back home, with the pandemic in full swing, I decided to invest in myself and my future by enrolling in Udacity's Data Analyst Nanodegree. It provided the structured learning that I craved and also allowed me to actively practice the concepts with the built in projects.

March 2021

Completed the Nanodegree

Kingston, Jamaica

I completed the program with 3 projects under my belt and the confidence I needed to put myself out there to seek internships. I updated my LinkedIn and continuously networked with professionals in the field. This meant scheduling coffee chats with people all over the world to learn about what a day in the life of a Data Analyst was like.

April 2021

Landed a remote internship!

Kingston, Jamaica

I scored an opportunity to work with a Canadian startup as a Data Analyst/Web Developer/Newsletter Manager. I got my first real world experience building dashboards in Tableau and presenting them to stakeholders.

Oct 2021

Secured my first full-time role!

Kingston, Jamaica

I finally landed my first full-time role as a Data Analyst at a local Auto Parts company, where I underwent tremendous personal and professional growth.

March 2022

Offered a remote role!

Kingston, Jamaica

Imagine my surprise when my role model in the industry contacted me about an opening at her BI consulting company! It was a goal of mine to transition into consulting and work remotely and I was offered both all in one! Now I work with companies to implement Business Intelligence systems that will provide exceptional value to not just executives, but everyone in the organziation.

Dec 2022

Passed the Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst Exam!

Kingston, Jamaica

On my second attempt, I passed the PL-300 Exam which boosted my confidence in my Power BI skills.
